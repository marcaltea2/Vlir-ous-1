Nicaragua

(ending as partner country after academic year 2021-2022)

Since 2003, we have spent over EUR 2 million in cooperation with Nicaragua. Following a few years without any project opportunities, Nicaragua returned to our list of partner countries in 2016 because of its high potential for our organisation. The country programme 2017-2021 for Nicaragua will be a kind of exploratory phase aiming at motivating researchers to submit project proposals.

Nicaragua is still one of our 29 scholarship countries.
